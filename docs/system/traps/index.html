Traps

Edit on GitHub

Overview

  • Traps are found on the ground and activate when stepped on.
    • They generally apply a negative effect, but some can be utilized to your advantage.
  • Traps are normally hidden, and are revealed when you step on them or do a normal attack above them.
    • They become visible if you eat Seewell Grass or equip a Trapper's Bracelet.
    • Spring Traps needed to escape item islands are always visible.

Trap List

Monster

Monster
Blast Trap Player Causes an explosion that affects 1 tile in every direction and halves HP. Walls and items in range of the explosion will vanish.
Monster
Explosion Trap Player Causes a massive explosion that affects 1 tile in every direction and leaves HP at 1. Walls and items in range of the explosion will vanish.
Monster

Monster Warps a monster somewhere on the floor.
Log Trap Player A log flies towards you from the left. Causes a little damage and knocks you back when it hits.
Monster A log flies towards a monster from the left. Causes a little damage and knocks them back when it hits.
Hunger Trap Player Decreases Fullness by 10.
Monster Decreases Attack Power.
Spinning Trap Player Inflicts you with Confused status, causing your movement and attack directions to become random.
Monster Inflicts a monster with Confused status, causing their movement and attack directions to become random.
Sleep Trap Player Inflicts you with Asleep status, making you unable to perform any actions.
Monster Inflicts a monster with Asleep status, making them unable to perform any actions.
Shadowbind Trap Player Inflicts you with Shadowbound status, rendering you unable to walk.
Monster Inflicts a monster with Shadowbound status, rendering them unable to walk.
Blinding Trap Player Inflicts you with Blind status, leaving you unable to discern terrain or other beings.
Monster Inflicts a monster with Blind status, leaving them unable to discern terrain or other beings.
Delusion Trap Player Inflicts you with Deluded status, making objects take on a strange appearance.
Monster Inflicts a monster with Deluded status, which may make them run away.
Onigiri Trap Player Inflicts you with Onigiri status, making you unable to use items or activate equipment.
Monster Inflicts the monster with Onigiri status, making them unable to use abilities, and changing their Attack Power and Defense Power to 1.
Far-throwing Trap Player Grants you Far-throwing status, making fired Arrows and thrown items fly indefinitely, passing through walls and beings.
Monster Grants a monster Far-throwing status, making fired Arrows and thrown items fly indefinitely, passing through walls and beings.
Sluggish Trap Player Decreases Action Speed by 1, causing you to move slower.
Monster Decreases Action Speed by 1, causing them to move slower.
Removal Trap Player Removes equipped Weapon, Shield, and Bracelet. Even Cursed equipment will be removed.
Monster Decreases Attack Power and Defense Power.
Rust Trap Player Makes equipped Weapon and Shield rust, decreasing Upgrade Value by 1.
Monster Decreases Defense Power. Some monsters will be inflicted with the same effect when getting wet.
Rotting Trap Player Causes all the Food you have to rot. Food inside Pot will not rot. When inflicted with Onigiri status, stepping on this will make you rot and collapse.
Monster Makes the monster become Rotten Onigiri.
Curse Trap Player Curses 1 of your belongings.
Monster Inflicts a monster with Sealed status, making them unable to use abilities.
Silver-seal Trap Player Makes 1 of your equipped Weapon or Shield Runes become silver-sealed, negating its effects.
Monster Inflicts a monster with Sealed status, making them unable to use abilities.
Summoning Trap Player Causes monsters to appear in the area. Breaks after triggering.
Monster
Alarm Switch Player Awakens monsters on the floor and cures their status ailments.
Monster
Rage Trap Player Grants monsters in the room Enraged status, increasing their Attack Power.
Monster
Monsterizing Trap Player Changes items on the ground in a room into monsters. Breaks after triggering.
Monster
Pitfall Trap Player Opens a hole, making you fall in and take a little damage. If there's another dungeon floor below, you'll land there.
Monster Opens a hole, making the monster fall down.
Floor-exiting Trap Player Moves you to the next floor.
Monster Makes a monster disappear from the floor.
Gassy Trap Player Makes you fart. Sends every being in the room running.
Monster Makes a monster fart. Sends every being in the room running.
Trap-setting Trap Player Places Traps throughout the floor. Breaks after triggering.
Monster
Feeding Trap Player Increases your Fullness and Max Fullness by 25. Breaks after triggering.
