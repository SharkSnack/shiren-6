NPCs

Edit on GitHub

Overview

Characters that can be found wandering around inside dungeons.
They're represented as a yellow dot on the map, and only appear in Table Mountain.

Characteristics:

  • Slow movement.
  • Can swap places with Shiren and ally characters.
  • Monsters won't attack them.

Ally characters can also appear as wandering NPCs, and can be recruited by talking.
NPCs turn hostile when you attack them under most circumstances.

NPC Floors Notes

Pekeji		 1-14F (Ally) Pekeji side quest.
Unlocked as Ally

Pay 200 Gitan to receive a massage with either a good or bad result.
Unlocked as Ally

Naoki		 1-2F Naoki side quest.
1st Encounter
Gives you a Bufu's Staff [0] and asks you to obtain Mamel Meat for him.
As thanks, he'll cook the meat and serve it to you.
2nd Encounter
Guide him to Mountaintop Town as part of the side quest to unlock a diner facility.

Lost Surala		 8-11F Lost Surala side quest.
Guide her to Cryptic Rock Valley to unlock the warehouse facility there.

Blinded Girl		 1-9F Throw Herb of Sight at her to cure her blindness and receive a kiss (Strength +1).
(Alternate dialogue: Throw Medicinal Herb, Restorative Herb, or Scroll of Need)

Kindly Man		 1-14F Talk to him when you're in need to receive a helpful item.
HP 10% - Medicinal Herb, Restorative Herb, Scroll of Need, or Chiropractic Jar.
Fullness 10% - Riceball, Big Riceball, Giant Riceball, Special Riceball, Riceball Scroll,
Hen Meat, or Egg Thing Meat.

Courier		 5-14F Offers to deliver an item to the Canyon Hamlet warehouse for free.
(The item is delivered instantly, so it won't be lost even if he collapses afterward)

Blacksmith		 8-14F Pay 1000 Gitan to increase a selected weapon's upgrade value by 1.
If you're lucky, upgrade value will be increased by 3 instead.

Riceball Man		 1-16F Rarely appears when you try to eat your last riceball while starving.
Gives you a Happiness Herb if you give him your last riceball, but otherwise collapses.
If he collapses, he'll reappear as Riceball Resentment to haunt you on the next floor
alongside lots of Polygon Spin monsters.
Riceball Resentment appears after the village if the next floor is a village.

Third
Apprentice		 5-14F Throw an identification related item (ex: Scroll of Identity) at him to help him out.
He can then be used to identify jars for free going forward.
Unlocked after seeing the Venting Jar Gaibara event.

Priest		 8-14F Throw a Scroll of Blessing at him to help him out.
He can then be used to remove curses from items for free going forward.

Kamahige		 10-14F Throw Herb of Sight at him to cure his blindness and receive a kiss (Strength -1).
He can then be used to identify bracelets for free going forward.

Wanderers		 1-14F 10 or so different NPCs who give you gameplay advice.
