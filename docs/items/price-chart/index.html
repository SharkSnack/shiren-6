Price Basics

Price Calculation

  • Selling Price
    • 40% of buying price.
  • Blessings and curses
    • Blessed → Regular price x2.0.
    • Cursed → Regular price x0.87.

Table Key

  • Red background : Always found cursed.
  • Blue background : Always found identified.
  • Yellow background : Prompts for "Which?" when used.
  • Purple price : Price when cursed.
  • Blessed items are simply double the price, so they are not listed.
    • Beware of the possibility of blessed Warp Grass (200) in shops.
